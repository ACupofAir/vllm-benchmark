The Kessler syndrome, also known as the Kessler effect, is a phenomenon that describes the accumulation of space debris in Earth's orbit, which can lead to a cascading effect of collisions between objects, causing more debris to be generated. This can create a dangerous environment for satellites and other space assets, potentially leading to the degradation of the overall space environment and the inability to use certain orbits for future launches.
